# ART DIRECTION
# ─────────────
# - Kid-friendly cartoon style (ages 5–8), bold outlines
# - Single subject centered on transparent or dark-friendly background
# - Subject should fit roughly square framing (1:1)
# - OUTLINE should match a simple silhouette — tracing dots sit on the edge
# - Bright, cheerful colors (purple/teal/orange accents OK)
# - No tiny background clutter — keep edges easy to trace
# - PNG, ~512×512 or 1024×1024
